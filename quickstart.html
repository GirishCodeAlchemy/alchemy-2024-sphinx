Quickstart¶

The Iron Man Suit package contains basic information about the three starter Iron Man suits built by Tony Stark at the beginning of the Iron Man movie series. These are detailed in API Documentation.

## About starter Iron Man suits

As you can see in the Starter Iron Man suits from the Iron Man movie series. image, these starter Iron Man suits have different types and abilities, and will upgrade into different suits as they are further developed.

Iron Man Suit details¶

For these three Iron Man suits, you can check out their details below.

  • Original Iron Man Suit

  • Armor: Heavy

  • Abilities: Armor, Flight

  • Upgraded Iron Man Suit

  • Armor: Enhanced

  • Abilities: Flight, Repulsor Beams

  • Advanced Iron Man Suit

  • Armor: Ultra

  • Abilities: Flight, Repulsor Beams, Strength

Usage¶

You can create an instance of Mark I called suit, for example, by doing

>>> import ironman
>>> suit = ironman.MarkI()
